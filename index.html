WELCOME TO PGDAY ARMENIA 2026

April 30, 2026
Armenia Marriott Hotel Yerevan

The #1 PostgreSQL Conference in Armenia

Join ∼200 attendees at the first-ever PGDay Armenia in Yerevan — a community-driven international conference inspired and organized by the Armenia PostgreSQL User Group, and supported by the leading companies in the industry. This one-day event brings together PostgreSQL users, developers, and contributors from around the world to connect, learn, and help shape the future of PostgreSQL.

SUBMIT YOUR TALK TO PGDAY ARMENIA 2026

Accepting speaker applications until March 8 (AoE)

Call for Papers is officially open! We’re excited to invite PostgreSQL professionals and enthusiasts to submit talk proposals.

We welcome speakers of all experience levels, from beginner to advanced, to submit proposals for technical talks, technical sessions, or lightning talks, and to share their experiences and insights.

We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment for both newcomers and experienced professionals. PGDay Armenia 2026 aims to be a fun, welcoming space where everyone can come together, share experiences, and learn from one another. We encourage you to submit your talk!

To submit a talk, please fill out the speaker application form. We can’t wait to hear your story!

SPONSOR PGDAY ARMENIA 2026

Accepting sponsorships

Sign up to become a sponsor and partner with us!

We’re carefully curating sponsor booths and non-exhibiting sponsors that are relevant, interesting, and connected to the PostgreSQL community. Our sponsors include tech companies of all sizes that build, operate, and innovate with PostgreSQL — from tools and infrastructure to services and extensions — actively supporting open source and shaping the future of PostgreSQL.

Interested in sponsoring and meeting real PostgreSQL users? Complete the sponsor application form and submit your application. We’ll review it and follow up with next steps.

Or, email us at pgdayarmenia@gmail.com with the sponsorship level you’re interested in, and we’ll guide you through the process. We can’t wait to partner with you!
